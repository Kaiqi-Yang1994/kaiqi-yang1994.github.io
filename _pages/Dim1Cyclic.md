The group $G=C_n$ is generated by

$$
\begin{align*}
G=\langle
\begin{pmatrix}
\zeta_n^s & 0 \\
0 & 1
\end{pmatrix}
\rangle,
\end{align*}
$$

where $\zeta_n$ is a nth root of unity, $a$ is coprime to $n$.

Here we take $n=3$ as an example, the magma code is as follows:
<pre>

n:=3;

FScale:=CyclotomicField(n:Sparse:=true);

F:=FScale;

Zn:=F.1;

s:=1;

G:=MatrixGroup<2,F|
[Zn^s,0, 0,1]>;

</pre>

$$
\begin{align*}

\end{align*}
